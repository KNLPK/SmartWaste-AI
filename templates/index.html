Background

PROBLEM

Waste is now one of the biggest challenges facing the world. Indonesia alone will produce 28.2 million tons of waste in 2023, with 38.12% of it not being managed properly.

One of the factors driving this problem is the excessive accumulation of trash in Final Disposal Sites. This happens because there is a lot of waste that should still be able to be recycled. The practice of sorting waste is still rarely carried out in Indonesia, making it difficult to process existing waste properly.
